Inquire Biology

An intelligent textbook that answers students’ questions, engages their interest, and improves their understanding.

Inquire was a research project combining a question-answering AI with a popular AP Biology textbook. I was product manager for a team of ten developers, researchers, and educators, as well as designer and developer of the iPad app. I later helped create an award-winning video and the project website.

  • A textbook chapter with student’s notes and highlights, plus the AI’s suggested questions and pop-up definitions.
  • Inquire AI creates a Wikipedia-style glossary page for every concept, with cross-references and related images.
  • Asking a question. Auto-complete suggestions illustrate the types of questions Inquire can answer and the best ways to phrase them.
  • An answer comparing two concepts. Key differences are shown first (e.g., functions); similar differences within a section are aligned (e.g., parts).
